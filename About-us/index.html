Our Journey

Founded in 2020 by education visionary Sonam Kumari, Nehra Education began with a simple mission: bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. What started with just 25 students and 2 programs has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving over 5000+ students across 15+ specialized programs.

Today, we stand as Noida's rising education destination, recognized for our innovative curriculum, industry partnerships, and exceptional placement record. Our commitment to quality education and student success remains unwavering.

About Founder – Sonam Kumari

Sonam Kumari is the visionary founder of Nehra Education, dedicated to empowering students through quality education and practical technical training. With a strong passion for learning and skill development, she established Nehra Education with the mission of helping students build successful careers in today's competitive world.

She completed her post-graduation MBA from Bangalore and has 8+ years of experience in the EdTech industry.

Under her leadership, Nehra Education provides a wide range of educational and technical courses designed to enhance both theoretical knowledge and practical industry skills. The institute focuses on career-oriented learning, professional growth, and preparing students for real-world opportunities.
